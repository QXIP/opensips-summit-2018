OpenSIPS Summit

Join fellow VoIP & RTC experts, developers and users from all over the world for 3+1 days of talks, inspiring presentations, workshops and trainings about OpenSIPS and the Open-Source ecosystem (Freeswitch, Asterisk, Homer, Janus and many more)

  • Learn about latest OpenSIPS built-in features and modules

  • Get project consultancy during the Free Design Clinics
  • Become an Expert attending the OpenSIPS Advanced Training

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not a developer. Is this conference for me?

Absolutely YES! Our conference is based on technical and telecom subjects, but with its absolutely laid back athmosphere it attempts to bring together and aggregate newcomers, intermediate and experts to exchange experience. Some developers will come to network, while other participants come to learn and we make sure there's always something for everyone.

I am not an OpenSIPS User. Will I feel excluded?

Absolutely Never! Everyone is welcome at the Summit to learn, teach or exchange their experiences. Our content will provide you with plenty of reasonsing, use-cases technical and otherwise, and material to learn more about OpenSIPS and its key features and streghts. Be warned as you might make a few Friends in the process.

I am a potential Participant or Sponsor. How do I reach the Organizers?

Get in touch today with summit@opensips.org to reach our leads or call our local partners @QXIP +31208080468 9-5 (CET). We'll get back at you in no time!
